Star Wars Outlaws

About the Game

Star Wars Outlaws is an action-adventure game set in the expansive Star Wars universe. Developed by Ubisoft, the game invites players to explore a galaxy far, far away as they navigate the underworld of the Star Wars universe. Encounter iconic characters, engage in epic space battles, and uncover the secrets of the Star Wars galaxy in a narrative-driven experience. The game promises stunning visuals, immersive storytelling, and thrilling gameplay that will captivate both Star Wars fans and newcomers alike.

System Requirements

  • Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 / AMD Radeon R9 380
    • Storage: 50 GB available space
  • Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700 / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1070 / AMD Radeon RX Vega 56
    • Storage: 50 GB available space
