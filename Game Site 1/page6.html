Black Myth: Wukong

About the Game

Black Myth: Wukong is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by Chinese indie developer Game Science. The game is based on the classic 16th-century Chinese novel Journey to the West. Players will take on the role of the Monkey King, Sun Wukong, and experience a rich and immersive world filled with legendary creatures, powerful enemies, and intricate combat mechanics. With stunning graphics powered by Unreal Engine 4, Black Myth: Wukong aims to deliver an unforgettable gaming experience.
