Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree Edition

About the Game

Winner of hundreds of accolades including The Game Awards Game of the Year and Golden Joystick Awards Ultimate Game of the Year, ELDEN RING is an award-winning action RPG set in a vast, dark fantasy world. Players will embark on an epic journey with the freedom to explore and the ability to experience the adventure at their own pace. The Shadow of the Erdtree expansion features an all-new story set in the Land of Shadow, filled with dangerous dungeons as well as new enemies, weapons, and equipment. Explore uncharted lands, encounter powerful foes, and experience the satisfaction of victory. Immerse yourself in compelling character interactions where drama and intrigue combine to create an immersive experience for you to savor and enjoy.

System Requirements

  • Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 10
    • Processor: INTEL CORE I5-8400 or AMD RYZEN 3 3300X
    • Memory: 12GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GEFORCE GTX 1060 3 GB or AMD RADEON RX 580 4 GB
    • Storage: 60 GB available space
  • Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 10/11
    • Processor: INTEL CORE I7-8700K or AMD RYZEN 5 3600X
    • Memory: 16GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GEFORCE GTX 1070 8 GB or AMD RADEON RX VEGA 56 8 GB
    • Storage: 60 GB available space
