ARK 2

About the Game

ARK 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the popular survival game ARK: Survival Evolved. Developed by Studio Wildcard, ARK 2 takes players on a new adventure with an enhanced storyline, improved graphics, and expanded gameplay mechanics. Set in a prehistoric world filled with dinosaurs and other prehistoric creatures, players will need to use their wits and skills to survive, build, and explore. With new features and challenges, ARK 2 promises to offer an even more immersive and exciting experience than its predecessor.

System Requirements

  • Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-6300
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 7870
    • Storage: 60 GB available space
  • Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1070 / AMD Radeon RX Vega 56
    • Storage: 60 GB available space
